Introduction ¶

Have you heard about the twitter account WeRateDogs ? Prabably yes, but if not. Here I will present a little bit about it and more than that I will show some funny analysis of some tweets data.

WeRateDogs is a twitter account that rates people's dog with a lot of humor, showing funny pictures and comments and a unique rating level. These ratings most of time have a denominator of 10, but the numerators are almost grather than 10.

This twitter page has over than 8 million followers and has received international media coverage. Its most famous meme is "they're good dogs Brent" and since November 2015 this page is filling his followers with joy.

So whats does this rating mean? Which dog type is most common in the tweets dataframe? What dog type has the highest average rating? These questions will be answered during this report.
